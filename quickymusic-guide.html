Quicky
MUSIC

Full-length songs in Second Life, played gapless from short clips.

Put music on it

  1. Cut your songs into clips with the Music Slicer tool (opens in any browser, no install). Drop in a song, trim off any junk, hit Export.
  2. Unzip the download. The .wav clips are right inside, already named correctly.
  3. In Second Life, bulk-upload the .wav files of a song (Build, Upload, Bulk).
  4. Drag all the uploaded clips into the player's Contents. It registers them by itself, tidies up, and the song appears in the list. Repeat for more songs.
Clips are stored inside the player and delete themselves from its Contents once they are safely stored. The kB readout shows how much of the 127 kB storage is in use - long songs take more of it.

The menu (touch it)

► PlayStart random play. Every song plays once before any repeat, even across restarts. While music is playing the same button shows ■ Stop.
» SkipJump to the next song.
≡ ListShow all songs in chat. Click a title to play it. Each line also has ✚ (queue it as the next song), ✘ (delete it, asks first) and ⇪ (export just that song).
▼ Vol-Volume down 10%.
▲ Vol+Volume up 10%.
⌕ SearchType part of a title, the match plays right away. Several matches come back as a clickable list.
⚡ ExtrasOpens the extras menu: Anims is below, FX toggles a soft particle shimmer around the speaker while music plays, Back returns.

Animations (optional)

Drop any animations into the player's Contents, then touch and open ⚡ Extras, Anims:

HoldPlays the whole time you wear the player (a hold pose). Opens a picker with the animations inside - pick one, or Clear to remove it.
PlayPlays only while music is playing (a performance). Same picker.
Both are optional. Leave them empty and nothing happens. If sitting down, standing up or a teleport interrupts an animation, it restarts by itself. Tip: upload poses with priority 4 or higher so furniture and AOs don't override them.
